Privacy Policy

Just Football · Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Just Football app ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. In short: the app collects no personal data of any kind.

Information we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking technologies, and no third-party SDKs. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you, your device, or your usage of the app.

How the app works

The app downloads the public schedule and results of the 2026 international football tournament over a secure (HTTPS) connection from our hosted database. This is read-only public sports information — team names, national flags, fixture dates, venues, and scores. It is cached on your device so the app works offline. No information about you is sent in these requests beyond what is technically required to make a standard, anonymous network request.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any data to begin with.

Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because the app collects no personal information, it collects none from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
