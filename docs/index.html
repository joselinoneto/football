FAQ

How often is match data updated?
Match schedules and results are refreshed regularly from our data source and cached on your device so the app works offline.

Does the app collect any of my data?
No. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. See our Privacy Policy for details.
